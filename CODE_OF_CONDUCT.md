# Code of Conduct

Kai follows a simple standard: be constructive, respectful, and careful with
security-sensitive information.

## Expected Behavior

- Treat other contributors with respect.
- Assume good intent, and ask clarifying questions before escalating conflict.
- Keep feedback specific, technical, and actionable.
- Be careful when discussing vulnerabilities, exploits, private repositories,
  logs, keys, and third-party systems.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Using project spaces to coordinate unauthorized access, exploitation, or
  abuse of third-party systems.
- Repeatedly derailing issues or pull requests after maintainers ask you to
  stop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or take other
reasonable action to keep the project healthy and safe.

To report a conduct concern, contact the maintainers privately. To report a
security vulnerability, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
